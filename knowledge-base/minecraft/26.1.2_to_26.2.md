## New gameplay content

26.2 (Chaos Cubed) adds sulfur and cinnabar content, the Sulfur Caves biome, Sulfur Cube entities, related blocks, items, tags, sounds, recipes, and world generation. These additions are content, not automatic migration rules.

Official release notes: https://www.minecraft.net/it-it/article/minecraft-java-edition-26-2

## Breaking technical changes (manual)

- Rendering backend: 26.2 adds an experimental Vulkan backend. Mods using raw OpenGL calls must migrate to Blaze3D abstractions.
- Registration/data generation: vanilla separates block and item ids into `BlockIds`, `BlockItemIds`, and `ItemIds`; `valueLookupBuilder` was removed.
- GUI/HUD: screen access moved from `Minecraft` to its `gui` object. Verified example: `Minecraft.getInstance().setScreen(...)` becomes `Minecraft.getInstance().gui.setScreen(...)`.
- Resource packs: final 26.2 resource-pack version is `88.0`; signs and hanging signs use block models and the `minecraft:signs` atlas was removed.
- Data packs: final 26.2 data-pack version is `107.1`; `#not_affected_by_geysers` was added.
